== Description ==
"All Social Share" is a WordPress plugin designed to enhance your website by providing easy social sharing options. With this plugin, users can quickly share content across various social media platforms, including Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, and more. This plugin is designed to be lightweight and simple to use, catering both to individual blogs and e-commerce sites aiming to increase their audience reach through social sharing.

== Installation ==
1. Upload the `all-social-share` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress.
3. Configure the plugin by navigating to Settings > All Social Share and adjusting the available options to suit your needs.

== Usage ==
After installation and configuration, social share buttons will automatically appear on the designated posts, products, or pages. You can customize the appearance and location of these buttons from the plugin settings page.

== List of External Services ==
This plugin makes use of several third-party services to enable content sharing. Below are the services used, their purpose, and privacy policies:

- **Facebook**
  - Purpose: Allows users to share content directly on their Facebook timeline.
  - Service URL: https://www.facebook.com/sharer/sharer.php
  - Privacy Policy: https://www.facebook.com/policy.php

- **Twitter**
  - Purpose: Enables users to tweet content from the website.
  - Service URL: https://twitter.com/intent/tweet
  - Privacy Policy: https://twitter.com/en/privacy

- **LinkedIn**
  - Purpose: Facilitates sharing content on LinkedIn profiles.
  - Service URL: https://www.linkedin.com/shareArticle
  - Privacy Policy: https://www.linkedin.com/legal/privacy-policy

- **Pinterest**
  - Purpose: Allows pinning images along with a description to Pinterest boards.
  - Service URL: https://pinterest.com/pin/create/button/
  - Privacy Policy: https://policy.pinterest.com/en/privacy-policy

- **WhatsApp**
  - Purpose: Enables sharing content through WhatsApp messages.
  - Service URL: https://api.whatsapp.com/send
  - Privacy Policy: https://www.whatsapp.com/legal/privacy-policy

- **Email**
  - Purpose: Offers a direct method to share content via email.
  - Service URL: mailto links (No specific URL needed)
  - Note: Typically handled by the user's default email client.

- **Gmail**
  - Purpose: Facilitates sending emails via Gmail.
  - Service URL: https://mail.google.com/mail/
  - Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

- **Telegram**
  - Purpose: Shares content directly to Telegram contacts or groups.
  - Service URL: https://t.me/share/url
  - Privacy Policy: https://telegram.org/privacy

- **Print**
  - Purpose: Provides a direct way to print content from the website.
  - Service URL: javascript:window.print()
  - Note: Handled locally by the user's browser, no external service involved.
